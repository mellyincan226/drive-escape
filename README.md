## 🧭 What this app does

drive-escape is a simple desktop app for planning weekend drives and short road trips. It helps you see drive times on a map, compare routes, and find places that fit your time window.

Use it when you want to:
- pick a road trip idea fast
- check how far you can drive in 1, 2, or 3 hours
- compare travel time before you leave
- spot better routes for a day trip or weekend plan

The app focuses on driving time, not just distance. That makes it easier to plan a trip that fits your day.
